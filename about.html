Team About

Increment Capital is a technology investment firm that fuses flexible capital with an active corporate development approach to investing across the capital structure in Asia and Asia-driven businesses in the US. Operating integrated businesses across private equity and private credit, the firm partners with exceptional founders and management teams with flexible capital to build enduring businesses over time with an emphasis on accelerating growth through strategic acquisitions, operational improvement and AI/technology adoption. Employing a sector driven approach, the firm invests in consumer technology, blockchain, artificial intelligence and AI/Digital Infrastructure.
